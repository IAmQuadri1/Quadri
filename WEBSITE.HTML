Our History

Barcelona is the vibrant capital of Catalonia in northeast Spain, known for its Gaudi architecture like the Sagrada Família, rich history, and a lively Mediterranean culture. It's a major global economic and tourist hub, with two official languages (Spanish and Catalan), a major seaport, and a thriving cultural scene blending historical charm with modern flair.
